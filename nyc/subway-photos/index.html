SUBWAY CONSTRUCTION PHOTOGRAPH COLLECTION, 1900-1950

The 20th century ushered in New York's underground subway system, starting at City Hall. Photographers captured streets (and tunnels) during construction, primarily for insurance. Recently digitized by the New-York Historical Society, the archive shows the city of the past. Zoom in to this map of automatically-geotagged photos and hover over points to see the images!
